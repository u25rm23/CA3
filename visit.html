Aberdeen Maritime Museum

Visit the Aberdeen Maritime Museum to find out about how the city used the sea. It is located near the harbour. The museum used to be located in the Provost Ross house which is the second oldest house in Aberdeen.

Slains Castle

Visit the ruins of Slains Castle which was used as inspiration for the novel Dracula.

The castle was built in 1597 and abandoned in 1925.
