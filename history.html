Aberdeen - 1179

"Bon Accord"

History Page

The city was first populated by hunter-gatherers 8000 years ago.

In 580, the first church was built by St Machar.

Aberdeen officially became a city with the charter of 1179.

By 1805, the main streets such as Union Street were built.
